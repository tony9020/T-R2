# T - Resume AI 📄

A high-fidelity AI-powered resume optimizer designed to ensure Global ATS compatibility and Harvard Business School standards.

## ✨ Features

- **AI Optimization**: Uses Gemini 3 Flash to analyze and optimize your resume content.
- **ATS Scoring**: Provides detailed feedback on ATS parameters and common errors.
- **Harvard Standard**: Automatically formats your resume to follow professional Harvard Business School standards.
- **High-Fidelity Export**: Export your optimized resume as a professional `.docx` file or a `.txt` file.
- **Real-time Refinement**: Refine your resume with natural language instructions.
- **Role-Specific Optimization**: Select your target role (Designer, Developer, Analyst, etc.) for tailored optimization.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Lucide React.
- **Backend**: Express (Node.js).
- **AI**: Google Gemini 3 Flash API.
- **Document Generation**: `docx` library for professional Word document export.
- **Text Extraction**: `mammoth` for parsing `.docx` files.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Upload**: Drag and drop your current resume (.docx or .txt).
2. **Select Role**: Choose your target professional field.
3. **Optimize**: Let the AI analyze and rewrite your resume.
4. **Refine**: Use the "Refine Results" input to make specific adjustments.
5. **Export**: Download your new, ATS-ready resume.
